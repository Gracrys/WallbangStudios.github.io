Description

Catarsis is a Post-Apocaliptic Shoot n' fight where you live the broken politics and fight against the humans who transforms in their worst fears, as you try to recover any sign of identity within yourself.

History

Catarsis was conceived in 2016 as a small project by a group of Venezuelan university students, we decided to call our team “Wallbang Studios”. The game was developed as a hobby and as a medium to learn, but slowly, the world of Catarsis was taking shape into something new and unique.

Then, the development had a crisis, many members left due to economic reasons and time issues, but the ones that remain, continue working and investing their time into our project. The game evolved remarkably. Later, Robert Nekrasov and Pamela Medina were incorporated, with the vision of achieving our goal: The Kickstarter campaign.

Despite the economic difficulties of our country, we’ve fought hard for this project. Catarsis has become the reflection of what we see. Our hard work against all odds was not easy, our extra low budget and the whole economy against us, wont take down our love for what we do.
